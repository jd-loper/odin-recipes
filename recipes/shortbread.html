Recipe for Shortbread Cookies

Easy recipe for delicious cookies.

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup white rice flour

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F.
  2. Line baking sheet with greaseproof paper. Sift flour and rice flour into medium mixing bowl. Add the sugar and mix.
  3. Cut butter into pieces and rub into flour with fingers. Knead into soft dough.
  4. Roll dough into an 8 inch round. Place on baking sheet. Using a fork, prick top and make tine marks along edge. Using a knife, score top with wedge marks.
  5. Bake 45 minutes. Sprinkle superfine sugar over top and let cool. Cut into wedges. Keep for weeks in airtight tin.
