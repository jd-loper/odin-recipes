/ lasagna

ingredients
  • 10 ounces lasagna noodles
  • 1.5 pounds lean ground beef
  • 3 cups cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 tablespoons dried parsley
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 can tomato paste (6 ounce)
  • 2 cans tomato sauce (6.5 ounce)
directions
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Bring large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 8-10 minutes; drain.
  3. In a skillet, brown ground beef. Using a fork, mix cottage cheese, eggs, garlic powder, parsley, salt and pepper. In a seperate bowl combine tomato paste and sauce.
  4. In a 9x13 pan, layer noodles, ground beef, tomato mixture and cottage cheese mixture. Two layers each will fit. Cover with tin foil and bake for 30 minutes. Remove foil for the last 8 minutes of baking time.
